Sabotage Connect 4

Rules

Connect 4 pieces of the same color in a row/column/diagonal to win.


Each team has a spy, who has the pieces of the opposite color. For example, the RED spy has YELLOW pieces, but is trying to help the RED team win.


Player order is Red Normal, Yellow Spy, Yellow Normal, Red Spy, and then it repeats.
